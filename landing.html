Features & benefits of our website

Search according to aesthetics

Our website allows you to filter your searches so you only see items that match the exact aesthetic you love, over time you automatically get recommended items you might be interested in.

Chat with the vendor

GirlyPop gives you the option to chat with the vendor of the item of your liking. This allows you to possibly negotioate prices, confirm details of items and possibly even get future items reserved for yourself.

Feedback system for user safety

Asides from the rating system that allows users to see and determine what vendors are most reliable and valuable, our customer service team is working round the clock to make sure your needs and questions are always met with helpful solutions.

A women only space

GirlyPop is a space by the girls, for the girls. It allows women and femme presenting people to shop comfortably and safely in a space made specially for them.
